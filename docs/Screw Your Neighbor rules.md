# Screw Your Neighbor rules

## Introduction 

Screw Your Neighbour, or Ranter-Go-Round, is a classic card game for 3 or more players. The game requires a standard 52 playing card deck and is suitable for ages 6 and up. In Screw Your Neighbor, Aces are low and Kings are high. The objective of Screw Your Neighbor is to NOT have the lowest card value.

## Set Up

To set up a game of Screw Your Neighbor, players need to form a circle around a stable playing area. Every player gets three lives (stacks of chips) at the start of the game. The dealer then shuffles the deck and passes out one card, faced down, for each player.

## How to Play

Gameplay begins with the player left of the dealer. The first person looks at their card and can either keep it or switch cards with the player to their left. Switches are permanent. Play then proceeds clockwise with each player having the opportunity to switch cards with the person on their left. If a player has a king, they must immediately flip it over. A player cannot swap with a King. A player who is to the right of a King must keep their card. The last player alive (with chips) wins.
